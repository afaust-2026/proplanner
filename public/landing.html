How It Works

Set up in under
10 minutes.

1

Create your account & set your profile

Tell ProPlan Scholar your degree level, university, whether you're an athlete, in Greek life, or a working professional. A quick 5-step onboarding wizard handles everything.

2

Upload your syllabi

Drop in your PDF syllabi and our AI extracts every assignment, due date, professor, and class schedule automatically. One upload per course and your entire semester is planned.

3

Configure your commitments

Add your work schedule, practice times, chapter meeting days, or any recurring commitment. ProPlan Scholar uses these to find your actual available study windows.

Let ProPlan Scholar do the planning

Your calendar fills automatically with color-coded study sessions at exact times — before work, after practice, between meetings. Nothing overlaps. Nothing is forgotten.
