Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 8, 2026  ·  Last Updated: April 8, 2026

The short version: ProPlan Scholar collects only the information necessary to provide you with an academic planning service. We do not sell your personal data. We do not share it with advertisers. You own your data and can delete it at any time.

1. Who We Are

ProPlan Scholar ("we," "our," or "us") is an AI-powered academic planning application available at academicplan.pro. We are based in Texas, United States. You can reach us at hello@proplanscholar.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you create an account and use ProPlan Scholar:

  • Account information: Your name and email address when you register
  • Profile information: Your degree level, university, and whether you are a student-athlete, Greek life member, or working professional — used to personalize your study schedule
  • Academic data: Course names, assignment titles, due dates, professor names, and estimated study hours — entered manually or extracted from syllabi you upload
  • Schedule data: Work hours, activity blocks (sports, Greek life), travel dates, and class times — used to generate your study schedule
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, and session duration — collected to improve the product
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, and IP address — collected automatically for security and analytics

We do not collect your GPA, grades, financial information, social security number, or any government-issued identification.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Generate personalized study schedules and academic plans
  • Power AI features including syllabus parsing, flashcard generation, and study advice
  • Send transactional emails (account confirmation, password resets)
  • Improve and develop ProPlan Scholar's features
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our service

4. AI-Powered Features

ProPlan Scholar uses the Anthropic Claude API to power features including syllabus parsing, flashcard generation, study advice, and the AI chat assistant. When you use these features, relevant content (such as your syllabus text or your question to the AI assistant) is sent to Anthropic's servers for processing.

We do not use your personal academic data to train AI models. Anthropic's privacy policy governs their handling of API data — you can review it at anthropic.com/privacy.

Important: AI-generated content — including study schedules, flashcard suggestions, and academic advice — is provided as a planning tool only. It may not always be accurate. Always verify important academic dates with your official course syllabus and your university's records system.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We use Supabase (database and authentication), Anthropic (AI processing), and Vercel (hosting). These providers access data only as necessary to provide their services to us and are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Community features: Professor ratings you submit are visible to other ProPlan Scholar users at your institution. Your name is never displayed — ratings are anonymous.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users or the public.
  • Business transfers: If ProPlan Scholar is acquired or merged, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your data is subject to a different privacy policy.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Supabase's secure cloud infrastructure. We use row-level security to ensure each user can only access their own data. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. Connections are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

While we take security seriously, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data through your Settings page
  • Delete your account and all associated data by emailing hello@proplanscholar.com
  • Export your data — contact us and we will provide a copy in a portable format
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

If you are located in the European Union or California, you may have additional rights under GDPR or CCPA. Please contact us to exercise these rights.

9. Children's Privacy

ProPlan Scholar is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us personal information, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us at hello@proplanscholar.com.

10. Cookies and Tracking

ProPlan Scholar uses essential cookies and local storage to maintain your login session and remember your preferences (such as dark/light mode). We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or by displaying a notice in the app. Your continued use of ProPlan Scholar after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@proplanscholar.com
  • Website: academicplan.pro
